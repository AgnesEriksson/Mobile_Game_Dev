Project Title: Egg-Cellent
Date: 10th Jan 2025
Author: Agnes Eriksson

Description: Egg-Cellent is a casual match game inspired by Suika Game.
Match corresponding foods to get bigger foods and collect points.
 Watch Out When Matching Scrambled eggs!
Tilt your device to Make Matches when its Scramble Time!

Folder Structure
-Eggcellent- : Root folder
-Eggcellent-
	-build.apk-
	-ReadMe.txt- GamePlay Instructions and References

Launch instructions
Download the APK on your Android device.
Accept Any Prompts that may appear.
Enjoy the game.

Play Instructions
Drag or Tap on the screen to drop Foods.
Match Foods To Collect Points.
If you Match Scrambled Eggs Tilt your phone to make extra matches.
Be careful to not overflow the container or its Game Over.
Navigate the Pause Menu to:
-Restart
-Go To Home Screen
-Access Volume Settings
-Resume
